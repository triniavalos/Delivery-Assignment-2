Cristian Mohaded

The Argentinian designer approaches his work as a dialogue — between materials and ideas, between history and the future, between utility and emotion.

Industry

Industry is the engine that brings ideas to life at scale. In this space, he explores design as problem-solving: focusing on usability, manufacturability, and material optimization.

Tradition

Tradition grounds his practice. It reminds him that every object tells a story — of a culture, a place, a gesture.

Innovation

Innovation begins with curiosity. He is constantly exploring new forms, functions, and materials that challenge the familiar. Cristian believes in using design to imagine more adaptable, conscious, and dynamic ways of living.

Stories

A collection of small moments, field notes, and memories — where objects are more than just things, and design becomes a way of telling stories.
